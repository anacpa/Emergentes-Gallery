Informations

The word Emergentes (emerging in English) comes from the idea of growth and evolution, contributing to the artistic world. It's something on the rise.

We are an exhibition center located in Coimbra. We aim to introduce the public to artists who are not widely recognised to promote their works.

This project started with two colleagues who created a website for a university course, which, in this case, had the idea of building a platform designed to give visibility to hidden talents and provide a robust platform for artistic expression.

The ability to explore such a diversity of themes, eras, and artists demonstrates numerous opportunities for learning and establishing a deeper connection with our inner selves. In this way, we strengthen our way of thinking and our critical opinions.

All images categorized by Classic Art on the site have an open access policy, making them available to download and be used freely for any purpose, including commercial. But all artwork in the Modern Art section is under copyright and should only be used for informational or educational purposes under fair use guidelines.
