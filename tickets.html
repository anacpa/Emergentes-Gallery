Accessibility

Art is about feelings and expression, something that's very meaningful. Accessibility is very important to us and our mission is to create a magnificent experience in the center for everybody. For that to happen, our establishment it's capable of all your needs and more. With ramps and specialist guides (as for deaf people, people who are hard of hearing, people who are sensitive to stimuli, and many others).

If you have any questions or dilemmas about this subject, please contact us.
